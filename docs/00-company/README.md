This section is the Company Handbook for Clada Systems. It explains why the company exists, how the company thinks, who it serves, where it competes, and how company-level decisions should be made.

The Company Handbook sits under the [Constitution](../CONSTITUTION.md) and [The Clada Way](../THE_CLADA_WAY.md). It should be read before making product, positioning, market, hiring, partnership, or long-term platform decisions.

## Company Definition

Clada Systems is a professional Irish software company building operational software for contractor-led businesses.

Clada OS is the platform. SolarGRANT Pro is the first product module on Clada OS. SolarGRANT Pro serves the Irish solar market first because that market has concrete operational pain: lead qualification, grant eligibility, document handling, installer coordination, customer communication, and human-reviewed submission workflows.

## Strategic Position

The company is not a single solar grant tool. SolarGRANT Pro is the focused entry point that lets Clada Systems learn from real customer operations while building reusable Clada OS capabilities.

Future contractor-led industries should be considered in strategy and architecture, but solar remains the sole implementation focus until product-market fit has been achieved.

## Operating Assumptions

- Contractor-led businesses need software that reflects field reality, not generic SaaS workflows.
- Operational trust is as important as feature breadth.
- Documentation, auditability, and professional workflow design are competitive advantages.
- Expansion beyond solar should be enabled by reusable platform capabilities, not forced by speculative abstraction.

## How To Use This Handbook

Use this handbook to answer company-level questions before moving into product or engineering detail:

1. Does the proposed work strengthen Clada Systems, Clada OS, or SolarGRANT Pro in the correct order?
2. Is the work grounded in customer reality rather than internal preference?
3. Does the decision preserve trust, simplicity, and operational usefulness?
4. Does the work help solar installers now while protecting future platform reuse?
5. Is the terminology consistent with [ADR-0001](../05-decisions/ADR-0001-clada-os-terminology.md)?
