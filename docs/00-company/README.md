This section records the company context that should shape product and engineering decisions.

## Company Definition

Clada Systems builds Clada OS, an operating platform for contractor-led businesses. The company starts with the Irish solar industry because it has clear operational pain: lead qualification, grant eligibility, document handling, installer coordination, customer communication, and human-reviewed submission workflows.

## Strategic Position

The company is not a single solar grant tool. SolarGRANT Pro is the first product module because it gives Clada Systems a focused market entry point and a concrete workflow from which platform capabilities can emerge.

## Operating Assumptions

- Contractor-led businesses need software that reflects field reality, not generic SaaS workflows.
- Operational trust is as important as feature breadth.
- Documentation, auditability, and professional workflow design are competitive advantages.
- Expansion beyond solar should be enabled by reusable platform capabilities, not forced by speculative abstraction.
