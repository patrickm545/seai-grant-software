## Purpose

Milestone 2 creates the Company Handbook for Clada Systems.

The handbook explains why Clada Systems exists, how the company thinks, who it serves, how it positions itself, how it understands competitors, and how terminology should remain consistent across the repository.

## Scope

This milestone is documentation-only.

In scope:

- canonical Company Handbook documents in `docs/00-company`;
- company vision, mission, values, strategy, and positioning;
- target customer definition and buyer personas;
- competitor category analysis;
- controlled company and platform vocabulary;
- navigation updates across the Clada Operating Manual;
- preservation of the Clada Systems, Clada OS, and SolarGRANT Pro distinction.

Out of scope:

- application behaviour changes;
- product feature implementation;
- UI redesign;
- future industry modules;
- broad codebase renaming.

## Success Criteria

Milestone 2 is successful when:

- future contributors can understand Clada Systems without private context;
- company-level documents are specific enough to guide product and engineering decisions;
- SolarGRANT Pro is consistently described as the first Clada OS module;
- Clada OS is consistently described as the platform;
- company positioning avoids hype, generic SaaS language, and AI-first marketing;
- customer and competitor analysis helps prioritise practical product work;
- all new handbook documents contain metadata and valid cross references.

## Handoff To Milestone 3

Milestone 3 should convert the company-level handbook into sharper product planning. Recommended focus areas include:

- Product Handbook expansion for SolarGRANT Pro workflows;
- feature-spec priorities for lead intake, qualification, document review, and installer operations;
- research plan for Irish solar installer customer discovery;
- definition of product-market fit signals for SolarGRANT Pro;
- ADRs for any platform capabilities that should be extracted from the module.
