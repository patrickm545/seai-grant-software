This standard defines how research should support product and engineering decisions.

## Required Sections

Research documents should include:

1. question being answered,
2. evidence gathered,
3. source or method,
4. limitations,
5. findings,
6. implications,
7. recommended next action,
8. links to dependent feature specs or ADRs.

## Evidence Quality

Use the strongest practical evidence for the decision. Customer workflows, support issues, compliance requirements, and direct operational observations should carry more weight than assumptions.

## AI-Assisted Research

AI may help summarise or organise research, but source quality must remain visible. Do not treat AI-generated statements as evidence without a verifiable source or human observation.
